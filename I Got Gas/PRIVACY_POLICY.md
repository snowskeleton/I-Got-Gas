# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 2026*

## Overview

I Got Gas is designed with your privacy in mind. Your vehicle and fuel data belongs to you. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

## Data Stored on Your Device

All vehicle, fuel, maintenance, and scheduled service data is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to create an account and enable sync.

## Accounts & Sync

If you create an account, the following data is collected and stored on our server:

- **Email address** — Used for authentication via magic link sign-in. We do not use passwords.
- **Vehicle and service data** — Your cars, fuel entries, maintenance records, and scheduled services are synced to our server so you can access them across devices.

Synced data is associated with your account and is not shared with third parties. If you do not create an account, none of your data is sent to our server.

## Vehicle Sharing

If you use the vehicle sharing feature, the email address of the person you invite will be used solely to deliver the sharing invitation. Shared vehicle data is visible only to the owner and accepted collaborators.

## Analytics

The app uses a self-hosted instance of Aptabase to collect anonymous usage analytics, such as which screens are opened and how many vehicles or services you have. **No personally identifiable information is included in analytics data.**

- Analytics can be fully disabled in the app's settings at any time.
- An optional opt-in tracking identifier is available for troubleshooting specific issues at the developer's request. This identifier auto-expires after 2 days.
- Analytics are not collected in debug/development builds.

## Third Parties

No data is sold to or shared with any third party. Analytics are processed on our own self-hosted infrastructure, not by a third-party analytics provider.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your vehicle data at any time within the app. If you have an account and wish to have your server-side data deleted, contact the developer.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, reach out to the developer through the App Store listing.
